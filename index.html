The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhoud. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. possesing a great sense of smell and superior traking insticts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agriculture import and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in (33-41 cm)
  2. Weight - 22-25 lb (10.0-11.3kg)
  3. Coat -Short haired, hard coat of medium length
  4. Colour -Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life span - 12-15 years
